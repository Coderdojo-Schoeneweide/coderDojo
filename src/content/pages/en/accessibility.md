---
title: Accessibility
background: light
---

All areas of the library in Schöneweide are accessible to people with limited mobility. There is no audio description system. Some doors can be difficult to open.

We generally offer our workshops in German (spoken and written). Almost all mentors also speak English.

If you need support in other languages (e.g., Russian, Arabic) or additional assistance (e.g., screen readers), please let us know. We strive to make our workshops accessible to everyone.
